Our Story

STARTED IN
NASHVILLE. Built for events.

Pristine Custom Cards started with a simple question: why don't events have better keepsakes? Generic photo booth strips, forgettable swag bags, candy nobody eats. We knew we could do better.

We launched with a single printer and a vision for what live custom card printing could look like at a Nashville wedding. The reaction was immediate. People lined up. Guests who had never spoken to each other were trading cards, comparing designs, talking about the experience for months after.

Golden Hour Photo Booth came next — because we kept getting asked if we did photo booths. We didn't want to just offer *a* photo booth. We wanted to offer the best photo booth experience in Nashville. So we built it the same way we built the card printing: with obsessive attention to quality, branding, and the guest experience.

Today, Pristine is the umbrella brand. Custom cards, Golden Hour Photo Booth, and live event activations — all under one team, one standard, and one mission: make your guests feel like legends.
